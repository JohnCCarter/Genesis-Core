## Observed

### 1. The shared pocket itself is not the annual failure mode

The earlier year-group comparison already rejected the simple claim that negative years own a unique pocket that positive years do not.

Both groups still share:

- late low-zone concentration
- bars-`8+` concentration
- heavy `LONG -> NONE` suppression
- later `NONE -> LONG` continuation substitution

So the current failure-mode map rejects the lazy explanation:

- **“the pocket exists, therefore the year is bad.”**

### 2. The strongest annual anti-edge boundary is blocked-entry quality inversion

The blocked baseline-long cohort remains the clearest separator on the current retained proxy surface:

- negative years: blocked median `fwd_16 = +0.076866%`
- positive years: blocked median `fwd_16 = -0.261849%`

That means the annual anti-edge boundary appears when the router is still suppressing entries that no longer look clearly weak on the same descriptive proxy surface.

This is currently the strongest repeated failure boundary in the whole lane.

### 3. Control-negative annual periods are suppressor-heavier and drawdown-worse

The annual primary cohort adds a second failure-mode pattern.

The control-negative subjects (`2024`, `2019`) are the ones with:

- heavier `RI_no_trade_policy` mass
- heavier `AGED_WEAK_CONTINUATION_GUARD` concentration
- worse drawdown than control

That does **not** mean those suppressors are automatically wrong.
It means their concentration is part of the currently observed harmful neighborhood.

### 4. Absolute strength can still sit inside anti-edge territory

`2024` remains the most important warning period in the lane:

- strongest absolute full year
- but control-negative
- and drawdown-worse than control

So one of the canonical anti-edge boundaries is already explicit:

- **absolute top-line success does not guarantee edge versus control**

### 5. The month-sidecar anti-edge seam is local, not general

The frozen exact-subject triad shows a second, narrower anti-edge boundary.

The negative exact subject (`2018-03`) differs from the two positive exact subjects because it:

- releases earlier within the cluster
- has weaker decisive-row support
- stays more structurally different from baseline while the seam is active

This is useful, but still local.
It is not yet a portable month-wide rule.

### 6. One single-suppressor explanation is now weaker than the mixed-cohort explanation

The blocked-reason split already showed that the blocked cohort is mixed:

- displacement / handoff rows
- true suppressor rows

and that `insufficient_evidence` is currently the sharper suspect seam than a simple “aged-weak alone” explanation.

So the previous one-helper-first story is no longer the best live failure map.

## Inferred

### 1. The annual anti-edge boundary is qualitative, not merely structural

The current retained evidence supports a cleaner phrase for the annual harmful neighborhood:

> anti-edge begins where shared-pocket suppression remains active after the blocked baseline-long cohort stops looking clearly weak, especially when suppressor concentration rises and control-relative drawdown worsens.

That is more precise than the earlier pocket-presence story.

### 2. The month-level anti-edge seam likely sits in release quality, not seam existence

All three exact month subjects exercise the same seam.
The negative month differs in **how** the seam resolves locally, not in whether the seam is present.

## Unverified

- This slice does **not** prove that the blocked-cohort quality inversion is the sole annual failure boundary.
- This slice does **not** prove that the exact same local month seam generalizes outside the frozen triad.
- This slice does **not** prove one fill-aware or trade-realized causal mechanism.

## Consequence

Phase 4 is now complete.
The lane now has an explicit anti-edge map and three real boundary statements:

- absolute strength versus relative harm
- pocket presence versus pocket quality
- broad phase band versus local release structure

## What changed and what did not

What changed:

- the lane now has a bounded anti-edge map instead of only positive-versus-negative comparisons
- the strongest failure boundary is now frozen as blocked-entry quality inversion
- the simple unique-pocket and monotonic-diff stories are now explicitly rejected

What did **not** change:

- no runtime changes were made
- no single-cause failure mechanism was promoted as proven truth
