## Binding-gap reading guide

- `structurally_satisfiable` means the surface can be discussed honestly from current docs.
- `partially_satisfiable` means a policy or boundary is visible, but the carrier/binding side remains absent.
- `unresolved` means the required closure surface is named yet still open.
- `intentionally_blocked` means the only honest stance is to stop before runtime-facing meaning appears.
- `forbidden_to_infer` means the apparent shortcut would exceed repo-visible evidence.
