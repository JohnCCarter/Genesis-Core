## Observed

### 1. The grouped annual proxy split still remains the strongest retained support surface

The strongest direct support for the current label is unchanged:

Blocked baseline-long `fwd_16` proxy summary:

- negative full years: mean `+0.356117%`, median `+0.076866%`, `>0` share `51.08%`
- positive full years: mean `-0.172585%`, median `-0.261849%`, `>0` share `45.99%`

That is still the clearest repeated sign-separating annual proxy surface on the retained topology chain.

### 2. The boundary does not survive as a simple pocket-presence story

The shared late low-zone pocket remains present in both positive and negative year groups.
That broader explanation is still rejected.

So the current label, if it survives, must stay attached to:

- blocked-entry **quality inversion inside** the shared pocket,

not to the pocket alone.

### 3. The blocked boundary is real, but the blocked cohort is mixed

The blocked action-layer surface is not one clean suppressor seam.
The most important limiting facts remain:

- largest blocked bucket: `stable_continuation_state`
- other large blocked buckets: `AGED_WEAK_CONTINUATION_GUARD`, `insufficient_evidence`
- among true suppressors, `insufficient_evidence` is the sharper suspect

So the grouped boundary is real, but its inner carrier story is mixed from the start.

### 4. Exact-surface D1 work supports one narrow carrier family, but only as bounded evidence

The exact-surface D1 bank supports a recurring non-age `insufficient_evidence` family on fixed exact subjects using:

- `action_edge`
- `confidence_gate`
- `clarity_raw`

That matters because it gives one plausible carrier beneath the broader grouped boundary.
But the D1 bank remains:

- exact-surface only
- context-leaky
- non-authoritative

So it narrows the interpretation; it does not finish it.

### 5. The first fresh external falsifier breaks carrier transport, not the grouped annual proxy split itself

The first late-2024 external-surface falsifier showed:

- the frozen D1 bank remained internally coherent,
- but unchanged transport failed on the fresh late-2024 surface.

That is a serious limitation.
But it attacks:

- portable carrier transport,

not:

- the existence of the grouped annual quality inversion split itself.

### 6. The local harmful 2024 pocket may be broader than the narrow `insufficient_evidence` carrier

The fixed three-cohort discriminator isolated the harmful `2024` pocket not only by age-like ordering, but also by weaker local router-quality fields.

That implies the harmful neighborhood may be broader than just one `insufficient_evidence` carrier.
So the narrower carrier should not absorb the whole label.

## Inferred

### 1. The `ACTIVE` label still survives, but only at the grouped annual proxy level

The strongest support surface remains stronger than the limitations.
None of the challenge surfaces directly falsifies the grouped blocked-baseline-long annual split.

So the current best verdict is:

- keep the zone label `ACTIVE`
- narrow its wording to the grouped annual proxy boundary only

### 2. Carrier explanations should remain `WEAK`

The narrowing and challenge surfaces limit what can honestly sit under the label.

So the current carrier state should be:

- mixed blocked action-layer explanation -> `WEAK`
- narrow `insufficient_evidence` D1 carrier explanation -> `WEAK`
- broader local harmful 2024 shell explanation -> `WEAK`

### 3. The cleanest downgrade candidate was portability, not the annual grouped split

If the grouped annual split itself had collapsed, a downgrade to `WEAK` would be appropriate.
But the strongest collapse currently appears on transport of one narrow carrier family, not on the grouped proxy boundary that earned the original label.

## Unverified

- whether a second fresh external falsifier would keep the same verdict balance
- whether one stronger carrier explanation can later be promoted above `WEAK`
- whether the grouped annual proxy boundary can ever be refined into a more portable subject-relative rule without reopening new bounded work

## Verdict

### Label outcome

- `blocked_baseline_long_quality_inversion_boundary` -> **keep `ACTIVE`**

### Required narrowing

Interpret the label only as:

> the strongest currently retained **grouped annual proxy boundary** where blocked baseline longs look materially less weak in negative full years than in positive full years inside the shared late low-zone pocket.

Do **not** currently interpret it as:

- a clean single-carrier rule
- a portable exact-surface transport rule
- a runtime target
- a global threshold law

### Carrier parking state

- mixed blocked action-layer explanation -> `WEAK`
- exact-surface `insufficient_evidence` D1 carrier -> `WEAK`
- broader local harmful `2024` shell -> `WEAK`
